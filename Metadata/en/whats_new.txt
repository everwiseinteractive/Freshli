Welcome to Freshli 1.0 — the future of tactile nutrition.

This is not a food tracker. It is the first app that lets you physically feel your ingredients.

LIQUID GLASS INTERFACE
Every surface in Freshli is rendered live by a Metal 4 refractive shader — your pantry breathes, your cards float, and ambient light bends through the interface like glass in sunlight. No static backgrounds. No flat UI. A living kitchen.

GAZE-ADAPTIVE RECIPES (Apple Intelligence)
Hold your attention on any recipe card for 0.6 seconds. Freshli's on-device intelligence detects your intent and the card blooms — refraction density deepens, a specular highlight tracks your device tilt via gyroscope, and a luminous rim traces the card edge. The app responds before you tap.

THE MELT TRANSITION
Switch tabs and the current view dissolves through a Metal 4 noise-field — pixels liquefying from top to bottom along an organic frontier edged in green light. Your device vibrates in sync: a continuous rumble softens as the view dissolves, then a sharp crystalline click as the new screen solidifies. Navigation has never felt like this.

HAPTIC VISCOSITY
Every animation is paired with a Core Haptics pattern tuned to mimic physical material. Glass forming. Liquid dissolving. Food being rescued.

RESCUE CHEF — ON DEVICE, ALWAYS PRIVATE
Apple Intelligence writes recipes for your exact pantry. Zero server calls. Your food data never leaves your phone.
